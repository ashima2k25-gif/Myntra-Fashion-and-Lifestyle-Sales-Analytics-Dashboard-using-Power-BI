# Project Overview : 
* Built a 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard for e-commerce analysis
* Analyzed 121M+ total revenue across categories and brands
* Visualized original price vs discount price impact on revenue
* Studied customer reviews and ratings to compare brand performance
* Used slicers (Gender, Category, Brand, Price Category, Rating, Reviews) for dynamic filtering
* Delivered insights on top-selling products, pricing strategy, and customer engagement

****************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************
Key KPIs :
* Total Revenue
* Category-wise Revenue
* Brand-wise Sales
* Original Price vs Discount Price
* Customer Ratings & Reviews

************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************
Dashboard Insights :
* Indian Wear, Bottom Wear, and Sportswear contribute the highest revenue
* Top performing brands include Puma, HRX, HERE&NOW, Sangria, Roadster
* Discount price strongly influences customer purchase behavior
* Higher reviews correlate with stronger brand performance
* Category segments help identify profitable product groups
